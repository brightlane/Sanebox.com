Why Your Inbox Is Overwhelming

Most inboxes fail because they treat all emails equally. Newsletters, promotions, receipts, and important client emails all arrive in the same stream. This creates decision fatigue and constant distraction.

The real problem is not volume—it’s lack of prioritization.

Core Principles of Email Productivity

  • Not all emails deserve attention immediately
  • Your inbox should show only important messages
  • Automation beats manual sorting every time
  • Reducing interruptions improves focus drastically

How SaneBox Works

SaneBox analyzes your email history and identifies important senders and patterns. Everything else is moved into folders like:

  • SaneLater (low priority emails)
  • SaneNews (newsletters)
  • SaneBlackHole (unsubscribe-style filtering)

This means your inbox only contains what matters right now.

Advanced Productivity Tips

1. Only check email at scheduled times

Constant checking destroys focus. Limit email to 2–3 sessions per day.

2. Use “SaneBlackHole” aggressively

3. Archive aggressively

Your inbox is not storage—it is a workspace.

4. Combine with calendar blocks

Protect deep work time from interruptions.

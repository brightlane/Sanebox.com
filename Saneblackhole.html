What is SaneBlackHole?
The permanent email block that actually works.

SaneBlackHole is SaneBox's most powerful noise-elimination tool. Drag any sender there once and they can never reach your inbox again — permanently, automatically, with zero unsubscribe steps required.

How it works

One drag. Gone forever.

SaneBlackHole is a special folder SaneBox creates in your email client. When you drag any email into it, SaneBox permanently blocks all future emails from that sender's address and domain. They simply never arrive in your inbox again — automatically.

vs unsubscribing

Why it beats the unsubscribe button

Most unsubscribe flows take 3-5 clicks, require email confirmation, often result in more marketing emails, and don't stop all emails from that domain. SaneBlackHole requires one drag. No confirmation, no waiting, no follow-up spam. It just works.

First session

How to use it on day one

Go through your SaneLater and SaneNews folders. Every sender you will never need again — LinkedIn notifications, cold outreach sequences, SaaS tool digests, promotional emails — drag to SaneBlackHole. Most users eliminate 40-80 senders in under 10 minutes.

Safety net

It does not delete emails

SaneBlackHole archives emails silently — they are not deleted. You can still search for them if you ever need one. But they will never interrupt your inbox again. The block is permanent until you remove it, and completely reversible.

Power tip

You can drag entire domains to SaneBlackHole, not just individual senders. One drag on any LinkedIn notification blocks all LinkedIn emails permanently. One drag on a cold outreach email blocks that entire agency's domain.

What to send to SaneBlackHole

Good candidates include cold sales outreach you never asked for, LinkedIn and social media notification emails, newsletters you subscribed to years ago and never read, promotional emails from tools you no longer use, job board alerts that are no longer relevant, and any sender that has emailed you more than twice without you ever replying.
